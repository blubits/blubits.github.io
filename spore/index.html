Air pollution is a major problem in Metro Manila. According to a 2007 World Bank report, 5,000 people die prematurely due to diseases resulting from exposure to poor air quality in Metro Manila. Lost income was estimated at $134 million from air pollution-related deaths.

Introducing Spore

Spore is an environment tracker capable of analyzing the air around you and measuring concentrations of pollutants, specifically carbon dioxide and particulate matter.

Our Solution

Stay Connected

Spore streams the collected data to your phone and displays the information to your screen, in real-time.

Spore for Research

We believe in open data. Each Spore device contributes to a crowdsourced map of pollution in your area. By using Spore, you are joining a group of like-minded individuals, helping create the world a bettter place.
